Who we are?

Over the past 12 years since our inception, we at Dr. Raju's have guided students from diverse backgrounds to Universities all across the world. Students who come to us are not only seeking training in entrance tests such as GRE, TOEFL, IELTS etc.. but are also looking for guidance in the entire process- Test Prep to University Application. At Dr. Raju's we are building a platform where the students can get guidance at each and every step of their Journey abroad!

Every student who consults us, comes with a purpose of finding better Education, Economic realization, and Skill development. We keep this purpose at the fore-front of our decision making, through out the process of Coaching and University guidance.

Dr. Prasad Raju V.V.N.R Pathapati (aka Dr. Raju) received his B.E, M.E, MS, PhD degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Gulbarga, BITS (Pilani), University Of Connecticut (Storrs, US) and Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University (NIT-GEC, Raipur) respectively.

Dr. Raju is a well admired Professor & Scientist in the field of theoretical and dynamic modelling of Kinematics, Robotics and Fuel Cells.

He published and reviewed several papers in International journals such as ASME (American Society of Mechanical Engineers, US), SAE (Society for Automotive Engineers, Michigan US), Elsevier Science (Renewable Energy), etc. Dr Raju has also worked as a Director, Principal, Professor, Head of the Department and Visiting Research Scholar in Reputed Engineering Colleges and Universities in India and Abroad.

Dr. Raju has founded Dr Raju's Educational Academy in 2006 in order to provide GRE coaching and University guidance services, from his experience of pursuing Master’s in the USA. He has been relentlessly imparting his knowledge through GRE prep to over 20000+ students for over 14 years now, who were later guided by him to world renowned institutes like MIT, John Hopkins, U C Berkeley, U PENN, UCLA, Dartmouth, Brown, Columbia, Georgia Tech, Vanderbilt, North-western, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, UIUC, UT Austin, Purdue, PENN State, Cornell etc.,

He keeps immense knowledge regarding the wide ranging courses and programs in MS, PhD and MBA offered by different institutions in the US. Apart from teaching GRE verbal module, he also recommends university lists based on student’s profile.
